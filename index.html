About

Breast cancer is one of the most common cancers affecting women worldwide. Early detection is key to increasing survival rates. Thermography offers a non-invasive and radiation-free screening method by capturing the heat patterns of the breast.

Motivation

Traditional mammograms can be uncomfortable and expose patients to radiation. Thermal imaging is safer, quicker, and potentially more accessible, making it a compelling choice for early breast cancer detection.

Upload Thermal Image

Methodology

  1. User uploads a thermal breast image.
  2. The image is preprocessed for analysis.
  3. A trained model analyzes temperature distribution.
  4. Results are generated indicating if the image shows signs of cancer.
